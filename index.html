MyBigPlate ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MyBigPlate mobile application (the "App"). 2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register.

  • Order information: Food orders, delivery address, and payment details.
  • Ratings and reviews: Feedback you submit about restaurants or menu items.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
